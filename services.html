IT Staffing

Contract staffing
Place technical talent on a contract basis for defined periods. Ideal for project work, seasonal demand, or filling gaps without a long-term commitment. We handle payroll, benefits administration, and compliance so you can focus on delivery.

Contract-to-hire
Evaluate talent in role before converting to full-time. We manage the contract phase and support a smooth transition to direct employment when you are ready.

Direct hire
Permanent placements for full-time roles. We source, screen, and present candidates; you make the hiring decision and onboard as your employee. Our process is designed for speed and fit without sacrificing due diligence.
