About Us

We Get It Done For You

A US-based IT staffing and workforce solutions firm built on execution, reliability, and partnership. We are driven by a culture of purpose, shared values, and growth.

Our Mission

To deliver workforce solutions that help our clients achieve their goals through the right talent—placed quickly, managed professionally, and supported by a partner who gets it done.

Our Story

Built for Enterprise Delivery

Getiva Solutions is a US-based staffing and workforce solutions firm focused on IT staffing, consulting, and scalable talent delivery for organizations that demand quality and reliability.

Founded by Gayathri, our philosophy is simple: we get it done for you. We exist to remove the operational burden of finding, vetting, and placing talent—so you can focus on running your business.

We were built to serve mid-market and enterprise clients who need a delivery partner they can trust: one that combines rigorous recruiting, clear processes, and a commitment to long-term relationships.
