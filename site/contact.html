FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you fill a position?

We typically present qualified candidates within 24-48 hours for most IT roles. Complex or specialized positions may take 3-5 business days. Our pre-vetted talent network allows us to move quickly while maintaining quality.

What industries do you serve?

We specialize in Financial Services, Healthcare, Technology, Government, and Manufacturing. Our recruiters have deep expertise in each sector's specific requirements and compliance needs.

What types of positions do you fill?

We fill contract, contract-to-hire, and full-time positions across IT including software development, cloud/DevOps, data engineering, cybersecurity, project management, and business analysis.

How do I submit my resume?

You can submit your resume through our Careers page, email it directly to careers@getivasolutions.com, or use the contact form above. We'll add you to our talent network and reach out when matching opportunities arise.
