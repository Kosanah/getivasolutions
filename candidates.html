Candidates — Find Your Next Opportunity

Connect with contract and full-time opportunities at established organizations across the United States.

If you are an IT or technology professional looking for your next contract or full-time role, Getiva Solutions can connect you with opportunities at established organizations across the United States. We work with companies that value quality talent and long-term partnerships—and we treat our candidates as partners in that process.

Why Work With Us

  • Quality opportunities — We work with mid-market and enterprise clients who have real projects and real budgets. You get access to roles that are vetted and actively hiring.
  • Clear communication — We keep you informed about status, feedback, and next steps. No black holes; no runaround.
  • Respect for your time — We screen for fit before we submit you, so you are not sent into roles that do not match your skills or goals.
  • Contract and full-time options — Whether you prefer contract, contract-to-hire, or direct hire, we have opportunities across engagement types and industries.
  • Professional representation — We present you accurately and professionally to clients. Our reputation depends on placing people who perform—so we invest in presenting you well.

Career Support and Placement Process

  1. Connect — Submit your resume or create a profile. A recruiter will review your background and reach out if we have a match or want to learn more.
  2. Conversation — We discuss your skills, preferences, and goals so we can target the right roles and set expectations.
  3. Matching — When we find an opportunity that fits, we prepare you with role details, client context, and interview guidance.
  4. Support through offer — We coordinate interviews, relay feedback, and help with offer and start logistics so you can focus on performing.

We aim to make the process straightforward, transparent, and professional from first contact to start date.

Benefits of Partnering With Our Firm

  • Access to enterprise clients — Many of our opportunities are with name-brand organizations and serious technology initiatives.
  • Multiple engagement types — Contract, contract-to-hire, and direct hire so you can choose what fits your career stage.
  • Industry variety — Financial services, healthcare, technology, retail, manufacturing, and more.
  • Long-term relationship — We are interested in your career over time, not just one placement. Strong performers often get repeat opportunities and referrals.
  • Compliance and reliability — We operate with proper contracts, timely pay, and clear terms—so you can focus on the work.
